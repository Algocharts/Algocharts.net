About FreeTinycharts

FreeTinycharts is opensource and its licensed under MIT license (almost do what you want with it).

You can contribute or grab a copy of this software to start your own Tinyman charts service on FreeTinycharts github. Code is sometimes messy :D

Also all historical ASA price data is avaliable for download for free here in SQL format.(Updated often)

FreeTinycharts is fully free to use and is maintained by donations and voluntary support.

It respects your privacy and DOES NOT store any cookie on your computer.


FAQ

I made a new pool on Tinyman, when will charts appear on FreeTinycharts?

FreeTinycharts grabs new pools every day. Wait 24-36 hours and if it doesn't appear search me in Telegram

Charts are updated every 15 minutes, it could be more often?

I doubt it at least in this stage.

Charts are not updated or price is old

Nope. Prices are shown in UTC time. I'll will work on this, to show it in your local timezone.

How FreeTinycharts work?

Prices are grabbed using fabulous Tinyman Python API

How much it cost to maintain the service?

While it doesn't track too much data and has no much traffic, it cost about 60eur/year. With donations I've already received (24/10/2021), I can maintain it for more than a year.

Where it will have a mobile user interface?

It's difficult to show usable charts in a small screen. I'll stick to this simple, fast design if nothing else better appears. If you think you can do it better, you can contribute.

Are new functionalities being added?

Yes! Donate or buy my ASA to show support for them and encourage me to continue. FreeTinycharts is yet under active development.

There will be an API or a show-chart-in-your-page?

Yes! I developed Wordpress plugins before so you can expect it in the near future. Also an easy to grab live price API.

There is no price data before 20/10/2021

Yes. Anyways, Tinyman started that month so less than 10 days of data are lost.
