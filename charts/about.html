About FreeTinycharts

FreeTinycharts started as a hobbyist project only days after fabulous Tinyman AMM went live. Previous versions are opensourced under MIT License, free to download from github.

A simple but powerful, lightning fast GNU/Linux backend combined with high connectivity using IPv6 and HTTP2 quickly put FreeTinycharts months ahead of their competitors, as it could track all existing data about Algorand Standard Asset pricing avaliable.

Having stored nearly all Algorand Standard Asset price data since the beginning of the market, now FreeTinycharts aims to integrate all the ASA price data available in the world in one single point, while providing free charts and information of all existing assets in the Algorand ecosystem to all of its users.

With its flexible, lightweight, frameworkless backend FreeTinycharts will integrate all information from current and upcoming Algorand markets while keeping costs at bay and aligning with the environmental goals of the Algorand platform.
